Free Trial

All ComplyyCert plans include a 15-day free trial. You will not be charged during the trial period. You may cancel at any time before day 15 without incurring any charge.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account's billing settings. Upon cancellation, your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. We do not provide prorated refunds for unused days within a billing period.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis. We may issue a refund in the following situations:

  • You were charged after canceling your subscription due to a technical error on our part.
  • You were charged twice for the same billing period.
  • The Service was unavailable for an extended period due to issues on our end.

Non-Refundable Cases

We do not issue refunds for:

  • Failure to cancel before the free trial ends.
  • Unused days remaining in a billing period after cancellation.
  • Account suspension or termination due to violation of our Terms of Service.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact us at support@complyycert.com within 7 days of the charge, including your account email and the reason for your request. We will respond within 3 business days.

Payment Processing

All payments are processed by Paddle as our Merchant of Record. Approved refunds will be returned to your original payment method and may take 5–10 business days to appear depending on your bank or card issuer.
